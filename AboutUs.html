OUR HISTORY

Pampers is a beer project with combination of 2 product Michelob Ultra beers and Pampers.It was a great project worked by Saroj Sharma G
in the year 1950's. The beer has shown a great success with lot of problem faced by the founder. This beer was his home made
brewery which is his family recipe. It was his grandfather idea of making beer home. As a family Saroj took the company from
his father and developed the company in to a world best and tasty beer. The had only one recipe and Our founder added three more beer
recipe with light, Blue and Green.The beer is now one of the most brought beer for the occasion.
